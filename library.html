The Santa Cruz Public Library

At the Santa Cruz Public Library, I developed archival workflows and tracked team efficiency and progress through meetings and detailed logs. Some memorable materials I digitized were century old scrapbooks belonging to female naturalist, Laura Hecox and magazines documenting the 1970s Bay Area feminist and LGBTQ movements, carefully handling fragile materials,creating metadata, and uploading digitized assets to public digital collections for open access.
